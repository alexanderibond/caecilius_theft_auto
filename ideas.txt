To launch a pixel game, break your project into these essential stages:

Design your Core Loop: Define what the player does over and over again (e.g., jump on enemies,
collect coins, and reach the flag).

Create Placeholders: Start with simple blocks or shapes instead of detailed pixel art.
You can swap in your custom art later.

Build Levels: Use a Tilemap system to paint the background and ground platforms.

Animate the Hero: Draw simple frame-by-frame animations (e.g., Idle, Run, Jump)
on a sprite sheet and hook them to your character controller.
